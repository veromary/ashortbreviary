\vern{I will praise thee with uprightness of heart, * when I shall have learned the judgments of thy justice.}
\latin{Iustificatiónes tuas custódiam: * non me derelínquas usquequáque.}
\vern{I will keep thy justifications: * O! do not thou utterly forsake me.}
\latin{In quo córrigit adolescéntior viam suam? * In custodiéndo sermónes tuos.}
